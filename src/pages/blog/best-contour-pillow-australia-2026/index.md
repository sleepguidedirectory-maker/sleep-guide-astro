## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Best Contour Pillow in Australia

### 1\. Material Matters: Latex vs. Memory Foam vs. Gel

- **Memory Foam:** Molds to head/neck for personalised pressure relief, can retain heat. Look for high-density foam. Gel-infused or ventilated options are cooler. (e.g., Tempur, Newentor, Ecosa).

- **Latex:** Responsive, bouncy, durable. Natural latex is breathable, cooler, hypoallergenic, and dust mite resistant. Offers consistent support without sinking. (e.g., TLC Latex).

- **Gel:** Often combined with memory foam for added cooling properties, dissipating heat effectively. (e.g., Technogel).

For a deeper dive into materials, read our article on [Understanding Memory Foam vs. Latex Pillows](https://opal.google/_app/_app-sandbox/#material-matters).

### 2\. Profile (Height) and Firmness: Tailoring to Your Sleep Position

- **Side Sleepers:** Need higher loft (12cm+) and medium-firm to firm support to align head, shoulder, and spine.

- **Back Sleepers:** Require medium loft (7–12cm) and medium-firm support to maintain the neck's natural curve.

- **Stomach Sleepers:** Need very low loft (under 7cm) or no pillow to avoid neck strain. Contour pillows are generally not ideal for this position.

- **Body Frame:** Larger frames need higher profiles; smaller frames need lower profiles.

- **Firmness:** Personal preference varies from plush to firm. Many contour pillows are medium-firm.

### Q1: What is a contour pillow and how does it help with neck pain?

A contour pillow (cervical pillow) has an ergonomic, wave-like shape with a raised edge to support the neck's natural curve and a dip for the head. This maintains optimal spinal alignment, reducing strain on neck muscles and ligaments, which can alleviate neck pain, stiffness, and tension headaches, particularly for back and side sleepers.

### Q2: Is a memory foam contour pillow better than a latex contour pillow?

It depends on personal preference. Memory foam offers precise conforming and pressure relief but can retain heat. Latex is responsive, bouncy, durable, naturally cooler, and more breathable. Memory foam is ideal for a deep, custom mould; latex for a bouncier, cooler, responsive feel.

### Q3: How do I know what profile (height) of contour pillow is right for me?

The profile depends on your dominant sleeping position and body frame. Side sleepers generally need a higher profile (12cm+) to align the spine. Back sleepers need a medium profile (7–12cm) to support the neck's natural curve. Stomach sleepers need very low profiles. Adjustable pillows (Newentor, Ecosa) allow experimentation.
