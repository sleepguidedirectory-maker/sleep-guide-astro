What to Expect: Unboxing and Setup  
Step 1: Delivery Box arrives at your door. Driver leaves it there. No signature usually  
required in metro areas.  
Step 2: Get it to Your Room Boxes are surprisingly manageable. Most are 30-50kg  
depending on mattress type. Slide it on its end or get a hand if needed.

Step 3: Unbox Place the rolled mattress on your bed frame (or floor if you don't have a frame  
yet). Cut away the outer box and plastic carefully.  
Step 4: Unroll and Watch The mattress unrolls itself. It starts expanding immediately. This  
is oddly satisfying.  
Step 5: Wait Foam mattresses: 2-4 hours for 90% expansion Hybrids: 4-8 hours for full  
expansion Memory foam: 24 hours for complete expansion  
Step 6: Air it Out There's a mild "new mattress" smell. Open a window. It disappears within  
24-48 hours.  
Step 7: Sleep Most mattresses are safe to sleep on the same night. Dense memory foam  
benefits from a full 24 hours, but it's not mandatory.

Common Questions  
Are boxed mattresses lower quality? No. The compression doesn't damage the materials.  
Foam and springs are designed to be compressed and expand without losing quality. Many  
boxed mattresses are higher quality than showroom options because they cut out middlemen  
and retail markup.  
How long does expansion take? Foam mattresses: 2-4 hours Hybrids: 4-8 hours Dense  
memory foam: Up to 24 hours  
You can sleep on most the same day, but patience gets you better results.  
What about the smell? All new foam mattresses have a mild odor from off-gassing. It's not  
harmful—just foam materials settling. Open windows for 24 hours and it's gone. CertiPUR- 
US certified foams (most Australian brands) have low emissions.  
Can I return it in the box? No. Returns don't require re-boxing. The company arranges  
pickup. They donate the mattress to charity or recycle it. You never touch it again.  
Do I need a bed frame? Not immediately. You can put it on the floor temporarily. But long- 
term, you want:  
 Platform bed with slats (max 10cm apart)  
 Box spring  
 Adjustable base (check compatibility first)  
Avoid putting it directly on the floor permanently—reduces airflow and can cause moisture  
buildup.  
What if it arrives damaged? Rare, but it happens. Contact the company immediately with  
photos. They'll send a replacement or refund. Most brands are excellent about this.  
How long is delivery? Metro areas: 3-7 business days Regional areas: 7-14 business days

Most brands use Australian distribution centers, so delivery is fast.

Trial Periods: How They Actually Work  
Every brand here offers 30-120 night trials. Here's what that really means:  
The Break-In Period (Required) You must sleep on the mattress for at least 30 nights  
before returning. This isn't the company being difficult—your body genuinely needs time to  
adjust to new support.  
Week 1-2: Feels weird. Maybe too firm or too soft. Don't panic. Week 3-4: Starting to feel  
normal. Your muscles are adapting. Week 5-8: This is the real test. Is it better than your old  
mattress?  
If You Want to Return Contact customer service. They'll arrange a free pickup. The  
mattress gets donated (most brands work with charities). You get a full refund.  
You don't have to:  
 Re-box it  
 Deliver it anywhere  
 Pay return shipping  
 Explain why in detail (though feedback helps them improve)  
The Refund Processed within 1-2 weeks of pickup. Includes original delivery costs in most  
cases.  
Can You Exchange Instead? Most brands don't do direct exchanges. You return, get  
refunded, then order a different model. Exception: Sleeping Duck swaps foam layers without  
returns.
