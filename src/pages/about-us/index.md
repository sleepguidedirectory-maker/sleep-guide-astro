## Better Sleep, Minus the Hype.

Welcome to **Sleep Guide Australia**, your independent resource for navigating the confusing world of online mattresses, bedding, and sleep health.

### Why We Started

Buying a mattress used to be simple: you went to a store, lay on three beds, and picked one.

Today, Australians are bombarded with dozens of "Bed-in-a-Box" brands—Koala, Emma, Sleeping Duck, Ecosa—all claiming to be the _perfect_ mattress. They all have 5-star reviews, they all promise "cloud-like comfort," and they all offer 100-night trials.

**But they can't _all_ be the best.**

We started Sleep Guide Australia to cut through the marketing noise. We don't care about the fancy jargon or the viral Instagram ads. We care about one thing: **Does this mattress actually deliver a good night's sleep for the price?**

### How We Review

We analyze mattresses based on the specific needs of Australian sleepers. We know that a mattress in Brisbane needs to handle heat differently than a mattress in Hobart.

Our review process looks at:

- **True Firmness:** Not just what the label says, but how it feels for back, side, and stomach sleepers.

- **Heat Retention:** Critical for our Australian summers. We test for airflow and cooling tech.

- **Motion Isolation:** Because a restless partner shouldn't ruin your sleep.

- **Durability:** Will the foam hold up in 5 years, or will it sag?
